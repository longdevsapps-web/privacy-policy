Privacy Policy – Workout Tracker

We respect your privacy. This policy explains how our app handles your data.

# 1. Information We Collect

Workout sessions, exercises, sets, and progress photos are stored only on your device using local storage (Hive).

# 2. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide workout tracking and related features. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data.

# 3. Camera Permissions

The app requests camera access only to let you capture progress photos. Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded or shared by us.

# 4. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties.

# 5. User Controls

You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app or resetting it in device settings.

# 6. Changes to This Policy

If we introduce online or social features in the future, this policy will be updated to explain how your data is handled.

# 7. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at long.devs.apps@gmail.com.
